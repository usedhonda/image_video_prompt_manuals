# Audio Cues

Sound design keywords for video audio generation.

---

| Keyword | Effect | Use Case |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Foley Sounds | Subtle action sounds (footsteps, rustling) | Realism, character presence |
| Diegetic Sound | Sound from visible source in scene | In-room TV, car engine |
| Non-Diegetic Sound | Sound from outside scene (BGM, narration) | Emotional score, inner voice |
| Spatial Audio / Binaural | 3D directional sound | VR, horror (behind you), immersion |
| Reverb Tail | Echo after sound stops | Cathedral (long), closet (dead) |
| Low Frequency (LFE) | Deep bass/rumble | Machinery, explosions, giant footsteps |
| Ambient Drone | Sustained background tone without melody | Unease, space, deep sea |
| ASMR | Close-mic whispers, tapping, chewing | Close-ups, relaxation, texture |
| Soundscape | Environmental sound collection | Forest, crowd, airport, office |
| Muffled / Low-pass Filter | High frequencies cut (through wall/water) | Club exterior, underwater, fainting |
| Staccato | Short, sharp, choppy sounds | Gunfire, typewriter, tense BGM |
| Crescendo | Gradually increasing volume | Climax build-up, approaching danger |
