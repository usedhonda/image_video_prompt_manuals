# Camera Movement

Cinematographic camera work keywords for video generation.

---

| Keyword | Effect | Use Case |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Dolly Zoom (Vertigo Effect) | Camera moves in while zooming out | Psychological shock, realization |
| Rack Focus / Pull Focus | Focus shifts between foreground/background | Speaker switch, evidence reveal |
| Truck Left / Right | Camera moves parallel sideways | Walking alongside, landscape pan |
| Pedestal Up / Down | Camera height moves vertically | Building height, hidden reveal |
| Gimbal Stabilized | Floating, shake-free tracking | Smooth walk, sports tracking |
| Whip Pan | Ultra-fast pan with motion blur | Action transitions, quick turns |
| Orbit / Arc Shot | Camera circles around subject | Hero entrance, 360 product view |
| Crash Zoom / Snap Zoom | Instant extreme zoom in | Comic surprise, dramatic close-up |
| Dutch Angle | Intentionally tilted frame | Unease, madness, dynamic action |
| FPV Drone Fly-through | First-person drone through tight spaces | Building tours, canyon flight |
| Low Angle / Worm's Eye | Ground-level looking up | Giant/powerful subject, feet emphasis |
| Bullet Time | Time freezes while camera moves | Explosion moment, Matrix-style |
